Explain to me what a notary public is
assistant: A notary public is a person licensed by the state whose only job is to be an official witness when people sign important documents.

Here's what they actually do:

1. They check your ID to confirm you are who you say you are

3. They sign it themselves as a witness
4. They stamp it with their official seal
