
## Bambu Lab P1S Combo — Lukas's Setup
- Bought today (April 1) from Best Buy for $549 — P1S Combo with AMS
- Got a free Maker World Piano Music Box Kit (K015) as a bonus in the box
- Currently has 1 spool of green PLA loaded in AMS slot 4
- First print: benchy (test print via Bambu app)
- Key thing he learned: always start prints from the **Bambu app**, not the printer touchscreen
- AMS auto-retracts/loads filament between prints — fully automatic
- To swap colors: unload slot from app → swap spool → it auto-loads
